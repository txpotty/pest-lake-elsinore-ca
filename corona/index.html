Is It Time for Professional Pest Control in Corona? Recognizing the Signs

Sometimes, a pest problem is obvious, with visible rodents or swarms of insects. However, often, the signs of an infestation are more subtle and can easily be overlooked until the problem becomes significant. Recognizing these warning signs early on is crucial for prompt action, preventing further damage, and protecting your health. If you notice any of the following indicators, it might be time to call Lake Elsinore Pest Pros for expert Pest Control in Corona, CA.

  • Unusual Noises: Scratching, scurrying, or rustling sounds coming from walls, attics, or crawl spaces, particularly at night, can indicate the presence of rodents or other pests moving around. These noises are often more noticeable when your house is quiet. Don't dismiss strange sounds; they could be a clear signal of unwanted guests.
  • Droppings or Urine Odor: Finding small droppings in or around your home or business or noticing a persistent, musty odor are strong indicators of a pest infestation. The size and shape of the droppings can often help identify the type of pest present. These are often found in hidden areas like pantries, cupboards, or under sinks.
  • Gnaw Marks or Damage: Pests like rodents often gnaw on materials to wear down their teeth or to access food and shelter. Look for gnaw marks on wires, wood, cardboard boxes, or food packaging. This damage can also compromise the structural integrity of your property.
  • Visible Pests: The most obvious sign is seeing live pests in your home or business, especially during the day. While seeing one or two may not be a cause for immediate panic, regular sightings indicate a larger problem. Different pests are active at different times of the day and night.
  • Nesting Materials: Discovering shredded paper, fabric, insulation, or other materials gathered in secluded areas like attics, basements, or within walls suggests that pests are building nests. These nests are often a sign of a breeding population.
  • Unexplained Odors: Beyond urine, some pests emit distinct odors. For example, cockroaches can produce a musty smell, and rodent infestations can have a strong, unpleasant odor.
  • Damage to Plants or Garden: While not always linked to interior pests, damage to your landscaping, including chewed leaves, damaged roots, or tunnels in the lawn, can indicate the presence of outdoor pests that may eventually make their way inside.
  • Bite Marks or Skin Irritation: If you or your family members are experiencing unexplained bug bites or skin irritation, it could be a sign of pests like bed bugs or fleas. These types of infestations require professional and specialized treatment. Our bed bug extermination service can help.
  • Damaged Structures: Termite infestations, in particular, can cause significant structural damage that may not be immediately visible from the outside. Look for signs like buckling wood, sagging floors, or hollow sounds when tapping on wood. Our termite treatment is highly effective.
  • Trails or Pathways: Some pests, like ants or rodents, establish regular pathways between their living areas and food sources. These trails can be visible as lines of debris or disturbed dust along baseboards or walls.

Ignoring these signs can allow a pest problem to escalate, leading to more extensive damage, increased health risks, and higher treatment costs. If you suspect you have a pest issue, even if you're not entirely sure, it's always best to err on the side of caution and contact a professional pest control service.
